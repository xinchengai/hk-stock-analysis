# HK Industry Frameworks

Identify the company industry first, then apply the closest framework. Use only metrics that can be sourced reliably. If the industry is unclear, use the general framework and state the uncertainty.

## General Framework

- Revenue growth and margin trend.
- Profitability and cash-flow quality.
- Balance-sheet strength.
- Valuation versus growth and risk.
- Liquidity and corporate-governance/event risk.

## Banks

Key metrics:

- Net interest margin (NIM).
- Non-performing loan ratio.
- Provision coverage ratio.
- Cost-to-income ratio.
- CET1/capital adequacy ratio.
- Dividend payout and book-value growth.

## Insurance

Key metrics:

- New business value (NBV).
- Embedded value (EV).
- Value of new business margin.
- Solvency ratio.
- Investment yield.
- Agent/channel productivity.

## Property Developers

Key metrics:

- NAV discount.
- Net gearing.
- Contracted sales.
- Land bank quality.
- Interest coverage.
- Refinancing maturity wall.

## Property Management

Key metrics:

- GFA under management.
- Third-party project share.
- Collection rate.
- Gross margin trend.
- Related-party exposure to developers.

## Internet and Platform Companies

Key metrics:

- Revenue growth by segment.
- Adjusted operating profit or EBITDA.
- Free cash flow and FCF yield.
- Share buyback intensity.
- User growth, ARPU, GMV, take rate, or ad load where relevant.
- Regulatory and competition risk.

Guidance:

- PE and PB alone are often insufficient.
- For mature profitable platforms, focus on FCF, buybacks, and margin durability.
- For growth platforms, focus on monetization, user retention, and cash burn.

## Consumer and Retail

Key metrics:

- Same-store sales growth.
- Gross margin.
- Inventory turnover.
- Store count and channel mix.
- Brand/customer concentration.
- Rent and labor cost pressure.

## Apparel and Supply Chain

Key metrics:

- Revenue by customer/region.
- Gross margin.
- Customer concentration.
- Order book or procurement volume if disclosed.
- Inventory and receivables quality.
- Operating cash flow.

## Macau Gaming

Key metrics:

- Gross gaming revenue (GGR).
- Mass vs VIP mix.
- EBITDA margin.
- Hotel/retail/non-gaming contribution.
- License and regulatory risk.
- Mainland travel and consumption trends.

## Biotech and Healthcare

Key metrics:

- Pipeline stage and upcoming catalysts.
- Cash runway.
- R&D expense.
- Commercialized product sales.
- Regulatory approvals.
- Partnering/licensing income.

## Industrials and Manufacturing

Key metrics:

- Order book.
- Capacity utilization.
- Gross margin and input costs.
- Customer concentration.
- Export/geography exposure.
- Capex and working capital cycle.

## Energy and Utilities

Key metrics:

- Commodity price sensitivity.
- Tariff/regulatory framework.
- Production volume or installed capacity.
- Unit cost.
- Dividend stability.
- Capex plan and leverage.
